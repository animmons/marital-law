We get Results.

We offer a variety of marital and family law services designed to meet our clients' needs. From complex business valuation to concerns about alimony and sensitive children's issues, we assist our clients as they go through the legal process - protecting their rights both now and in the future. Elite Representation

The dissolution of marriages with a large number of assets is often far too complex for an average divorce attorney to effectively handle. It takes the attention and skill of a lawyer with experience in high-asset divorce to protect clients' individual interests throughout this process.

At the Law Office of Benjamin T. Hodas, LLC, we offer elite and professional family law services for high-profile clients in West Palm Beach, Florida, and the surrounding metropolitan area. Our focus is on high-asset divorce and related legal mtters that involve asset protection, business valuation, Florida property division and more. We understand the complexities faced by our mostly wealthy clientele and work diligently to maximize positive results.
